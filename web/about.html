SUPERLATIVES

About

Superlatives is a synchronous social party game.

Each round has a category. Every player submits one entry for that category, then all players vote across several superlative prompts.

Scoring is proportional to vote share each prompt. The game works best with players who can talk to each other (in person or voice/video chat).

Clients:

  • Player client: submit entries and votes
  • Display client: shared-room board view
